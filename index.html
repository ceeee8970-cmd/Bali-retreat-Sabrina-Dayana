Day by Day

A journey through jungle, water and stillness.

Each day is gently choreographed — woven between practice, ceremony, exploration and meaningful rest.

Day One01

Arrival, Relaxation & Yoga Class

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport and be privately transferred to SereS Springs Resort & Spa | 5-Star, perched amidst lush greenery and serene rice terraces.

Settle into your tranquil surroundings, then ease into the journey with a gentle yoga class guided by Dayana Solano. As evening falls, an intimate welcome dinner in a stunning natural setting begins your time together.

Private transfer Yoga class Welcome dinner
Day Two02

Flower Opening Ceremony & Ubud Culture

Morning yoga and a curated breakfast open a day devoted to Bali's cultural heart. Take part in a graceful flower opening ceremony, then learn the delicate art of weaving Canang Sari — the island's daily offering of gratitude and devotion.

Afternoon leisure along Jalan Goutama for artisanal souvenirs, and at dusk, an enchanting Legong dance performance at the Ubud Water Palace — Balinese artistry at its most luminous.

Morning Yoga Water Temple Ubud Canang Sari Legong Dance
Day Three03

Sacred Waters, Rice Terraces & Elevated Indulgence

A revitalizing morning yoga practice followed by an indulgent breakfast sets the tone for a deeply nourishing day. The journey continues through Bali's sacred waters with a traditional Melukat purification ritual at the enchanting Griya Beji Waterfall — a spiritual cleansing ceremony rooted in Balinese tradition. The experience deepens with a guided meditation while contemplating the soothing sounds and beauty of the waterfall surroundings.

In the afternoon, enjoy a guided walk through the iconic Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Take a relaxing break at the renowned Tis Café, surrounded by breathtaking panoramic views over the terraces, before experiencing the famous Bali Swing soaring high above the lush rice fields. The adventure continues with a curated flying dress photoshoot — including professional photos and videos to capture the magic of the moment.

To complete the day, unwind with a luxurious 60-minute Balinese massage at the elegant Green Tara Spa, followed by a beautifully curated elevated group dinner shared together under the Bali evening atmosphere.

Morning Yoga Melukat ritual Waterfall meditation Tegallalang Bali Swing Balinese massage
Day Four04

East Bali Discovery

A nourishing breakfast before departing on a curated journey through the enchanting landscapes of East Bali. Begin at the majestic Lempuyang Temple, home to the iconic Gates of Heaven and breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Agung.

Continue to Tirta Gangga, the former royal water palace renowned for its elegant fountains, lush gardens, and sacred pools. The experience concludes at the hidden paradise of Blue Lagoon Beach — a peaceful secluded bay with crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming, snorkeling, or simply enjoying a moment of quiet reflection.

Return to the villa to close the day with a grounding sunset yoga practice designed to restore body and mind after a day of exploration. The evening continues with a beautifully curated elevated group dinner, sharing meaningful conversations and unforgettable moments under the magical Bali night atmosphere.

Lempuyang Temple Tirta Gangga Blue Lagoon Snorkeling Evening Yoga
Day Five05

Leisure & Optional North Bali Discovery

Our journey through the North of Bali begins with a visit to the iconic Pura Ulun Danu Beratan, a beautiful water temple set on the shores of Lake Beratan, surrounded by mountains and a peaceful atmosphere. On the way, we'll stop at a scenic viewpoint with the lake and mountains in the background — the perfect setting for elegant photos.

We then continue to the hidden Banyumala Waterfall, a stunning twin waterfall immersed in nature, ideal for a refreshing and magical moment. For lunch, we head to a hanging-style restaurant overlooking the breathtaking Sekumpul Waterfall, the highest waterfall in Bali — a truly unforgettable view.

On the way back, we'll enjoy sunset at the Jatiluwih Rice Terraces, before returning to Ubud for dinner.

Pura Ulun Danu Beratan Temple Banyumala Waterfall Hanging Restaurant Sekumpul Jatiluwih Rice Terraces
Day Six06

Closing Ritual, Flowers & Reflections

Begin your final day with gentle yoga, followed by a special sound healing session and a beautiful flower closing ceremony — a guided moment to honor the experiences lived, the connections created, and the growth embraced together.

A floating breakfast in the resort's peaceful surroundings, check-out, and a private transfer to the airport. Depart with cherished memories, a sense of renewal, and the essence of Bali quietly carried within.

Closing ceremony Sound Healing Floating Breakfast Private transfer

Frequently Asked

Questions, thoughtfully answered.

If something isn't covered below, our team is happy to walk you through every detail before you book.

What is the group size?

A minimum of 6 and up to 12 participants. This allows us to create a more personalized experience, adapting to your level and ensuring the right level of individual attention.

Will the host travel with us?

The host will guide the yoga sessions and be present throughout the journey. For organizational matters, the group leader Sabrina will handle all requests.

Do I need prior yoga experience?

No previous experience is required. All sessions are designed to be accessible and enjoyable for every level — gentle, grounding, and welcoming.

Can I join solo?

Absolutely. Many guests travel solo, and the experience is designed to foster connection, community, and meaningful relationships among like-minded people.

Is this trip suitable for solo travelers, couples, or friends?

Yes, absolutely. The experience is designed to be safe, supportive, and welcoming for solo travelers, couples, friends, and relatives. We welcome both female and male guests, creating a balanced, inclusive, and connected group atmosphere.

Can I have a private room?

Yes — private room options are available upon request, subject to availability. Contact us for details and pricing.

What language will the retreat be held in?

The retreat will be conducted in English, including all yoga sessions. A Spanish translator will be available throughout the experience to ensure clarity and comfort for Spanish-speaking guests.

Is travel insurance required?

We highly recommend purchasing travel and cancellation insurance to cover unexpected situations such as flight cancellations, health issues, personal emergencies, or travel disruptions. Insurance is the responsibility of each participant.

Can dietary needs be accommodated?

Yes — please share any dietary requirements in advance and we will do our best to accommodate them throughout meals and curated dining moments.

What about meals not included in the package?

For meals not included in the itinerary, we will accompany the group to a selection of carefully chosen restaurants, offering high-quality dining experiences in beautiful settings. Guests will also have the flexibility to explore independently if they prefer.

What should I pack?

Comfortable clothing for yoga, light breathable outfits, swimwear, and a few elegant pieces for dinners and special moments. A detailed packing list is shared before departure.

Is this retreat more relaxing or active?

It is a balanced experience combining wellness, cultural immersion, and relaxation, designed to nourish both body and mind.

How physically demanding is it?

Light to moderate. The retreat is designed to be accessible, with all activities optional. You're always encouraged to listen to your body and rest as needed.

What is the vibe of the group?

Our travelers are like-minded individuals seeking connection, personal growth, and meaningful experiences. The atmosphere is warm, inclusive, and supportive.

Will there be a group chat before the trip?

Yes — you'll be added to a private group before the retreat, plus pre-trip online sessions to answer questions and ensure you arrive feeling fully prepared and connected.

What makes this experience different from other yoga retreats?

This is not just a retreat, but a carefully curated journey that blends luxury, cultural immersion, and genuine human connection, creating a truly transformative experience.

Pricing & How to Book

How to book your spot.

Pricing varies by room type — see the accommodation section above to choose your room and reserve directly. Here's everything else you need to know about the booking process, deadlines and cancellation policy.

Booking process

  • Choose your room type from the accommodation section above.
  • Reserve your spot with a 25% deposit via Stripe.
  • Receive confirmation and a welcome pack with all retreat details.
  • Pay the remaining balance up to 20 days before the trip start date.
  • Join a private group chat and pre-trip online sessions before departure.

Payment methods

  • Stripe — secure card payment via direct link.
  • Bank transfer — international wire details on request.
  • Digital payment — additional options available.
  • Need an invoice? Just let us know when reserving.

Key deadlines

  • Reservation: 25% deposit due to confirm your place.
  • Final balance: Due 20 days before departure.
  • Minimum group: 6 participants — confirmed 20 days prior.
  • Refunds: Processed within 30–60 working days when applicable.

Cancellation & refund policy

  • Refunds. Processed within 30–60 working days, where applicable.
  • Deposit refund (25%). A 25% deposit is required to confirm room availability. If the room is not available, the full deposit will be refunded.
  • Trip cancellation by Sabriyoga Experience. If the trip is cancelled by Sabriyoga Experience due to not reaching the minimum group size of 6 participants, a 90% refund of the total amount paid will be issued if the cancellation is made up to 20 days before departure.
  • Cancellation by the participant. All payments are non-refundable. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made by the participant at any time before the trip.
  • Travel insurance. We highly recommend purchasing travel and cancellation insurance to cover unexpected situations such as flight cancellations, health issues, personal emergencies, or travel disruptions.

In case of unforeseen weather or government restrictions, certain activities may be modified or replaced.
